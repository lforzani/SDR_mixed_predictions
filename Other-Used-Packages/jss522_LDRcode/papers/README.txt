- DESCRIPTION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
This folder contains scripts to reproduce published results on sufficient dimension
reduction methods for normal inverse models. In particular, we give scripts to 
reproduce tables and figures in the following papers:


# JSS_example: examples in the manuscript for the Journal of Statistical Software.

# simulation-paper-cove: Cook, R. D. and Forzani, L. (2008a). 
Covariance reducing models: An alternative to spectral modelling of covariance 
matrices. Biometrika, Vol. 95(4), pp. 799-812.

# simulation-paper-pfc: Cook, R. D. and Forzani, L. (2009a). 
Principal fitted components in regression. Statistcal Science, Vol. 23(4), pp. 485-501.

# simulations-paper-cook: Cook, R. D. (2007). 
Fisher Lecture: Dimension reduction in regression (with discussion). 
Statistical Science, Vol. 22, pp. 1-26.

# simulations-paper-jasa: Cook, R. D. and Forzani, L. (2009b). 
Likelihood-based sufficient dimension reduction. Journal of the American Statistical 
Association, Vol. 104(485), pp. 197-208. doi:10.1198/jasa.2009.0106.
